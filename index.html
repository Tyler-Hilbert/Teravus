Miranda Baras

Co-Founder and CEO

Miranda Baras is a co-founder and healthcare technology entrepreneur with nearly a decade of experience working as a paramedic in emergency departments. Miranda made the switch from the bedside to the business side of healthcare after graduating from Georgia State University with a master’s degree in biomedical enterprise. She analyzes customer experience in the healthcare system and collaborates with industry professionals to provide innovative solutions that increase efficiency and improve patient outcomes. Miranda is an Atlanta native that resides in the metro area. When she is not working or participating in the thriving Atlanta startup ecosystem, she enjoys spending time volunteering with animal rescue, hiking, camping, and kayaking.
